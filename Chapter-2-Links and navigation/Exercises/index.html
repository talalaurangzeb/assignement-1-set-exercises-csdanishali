Suits Overview

Suits is a legal drama set in a top New York law firm, where sharp-witted lawyer Harvey Specter hires Mike Ross, a brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory. Despite not having a law degree, Mike proves to be a valuable asset — as long as they can keep his secret. The series blends courtroom battles, office politics, and personal drama with wit and style.

Cast & Crew

  • Gabriel Macht as Harvey Specter

  • Created by Aaron Korsh

Main Characters

  • Harvey Specter - The best closer in New York.
  • Michael Ross - A genius with a photographic memory who practices law without a degree.
  • Donna Paulsen - A sharp, confident legal secretary who knows everything and everyone.
  • Louis Litt - A brilliant, emotional lawyer with a hidden soft side.
